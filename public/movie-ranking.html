The Best Movies According to Ibrahim

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

A beautifully crafted movie showcasing opposing morals between characters.

A Silent Voice

A student with a hearing impairment is bullied and transfers to another school.
Years later, the former bully is tormented by his behaviour and sets out to make amends.
